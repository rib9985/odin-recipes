Vinagrete

Besides manioc farofa and Brazilian-style rice, the vinagrete is considered a staple, and the swiss-army-knife of brazilian side dishes. And a quite easy one to make. Throw it up in a quickie and serve alongside any dish listed here and you're in for a great crowd pleaser!

This recipe makes for around 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green or red bell pepper, diced (optional)
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
  2. Season with salt and pepper and serve.

Pairings

Well, tough luck. Just about anything, but especially salty dishes, main-courses, salads, and the world famous brazilian BBQ: churrasco
